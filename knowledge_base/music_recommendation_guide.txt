Chill / Study
- Usually has low energy and a calm mood
- Often instrumental or soft vocals
- Examples: Lo-fi, acoustic, ambient

Workout
- High energy and fast tempo
- Strong beats and motivating lyrics
- Examples: Hip-hop, EDM, pop

Sad / Emotional
- Slower tempo and low energy
- Emotional or deep lyrics
- Examples: R&B, acoustic, piano

Happy / Upbeat
- High energy and positive mood
- Catchy and lively
- Examples: Pop, dance

Focus
- Minimal distractions
- Steady rhythm
- Often instrumental
